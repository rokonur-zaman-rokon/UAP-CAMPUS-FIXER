UAP Campus Fixer

Your direct line to a better campus. **Report. Track. Resolve.**

Making Campus Management Transparent

UAP Campus Fixer is the Django-based platform designed to make issue reporting **fast, organized, and transparent**. We empower students to report problems—from a broken chair to slow internet—directly to the appropriate authority, ensuring quick resolution and a better learning environment for everyone.

Core Features

Issue Categorization

Easily tag your report (Maintenance, IT, Classroom, Safety) to ensure it reaches the right department instantly.

Real-Time Updates

Track the progress of your submitted issue from **Reported** to **In Progress** to **Resolved** in real-time.

Efficient Admin View

UAP authorities have a powerful dashboard to prioritize, assign, and manage all incoming reports efficiently.

Quick Resolution

By cutting out unnecessary delays, we ensure that issues are not just reported, but rapidly addressed and confirmed.

How It Works in 3 Simple Steps

1

Submit Report

Log in and fill out the simple form, add a photo, and select a category. It takes less than 60 seconds.

2

Authority Acts

The relevant UAP authority receives an immediate notification and assigns a maintenance or IT team member.

3

Track & Verify

Monitor status updates on your dashboard until the issue is marked as **Resolved**.
