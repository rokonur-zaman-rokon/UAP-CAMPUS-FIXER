Making Campus Management Transparent

UAP Campus Fixer is a Django-based platform helping students quickly report issues — from broken chairs to slow Wi-Fi — directly to the responsible authority, ensuring faster resolutions and a better learning environment.

Core Features

Issue Categorization

Tag reports by type (IT, Maintenance, Safety) for instant routing.

Real-Time Updates

Track progress from Reported → In Progress → Resolved.

Admin Dashboard

Authorities manage, assign, and prioritize reports efficiently.

Quick Resolution

Fast, organized issue management — no delays or confusion.
